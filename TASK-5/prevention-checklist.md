# Stay Safe Online -- Prevention Checklist

-   Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone
-   Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
-   Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be officials
-   Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
-   Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
-   Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions

## UPI / Financial Safety Tips:

-   Never approve "collect request" payments from unknown users
-   Always double-check the receiver's UPI ID before sending money

## Reporting Cybercrime:

-   National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
-   Helpline Number: 1930
