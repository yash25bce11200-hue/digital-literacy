# digital-literacy
For this task, I used Canva to design my digital literacy infographic. Canva is a user-friendly design tool that provides various templates, icons, and visual elements, which helped me create an attractive and organized one-page infographic. My design explains the concept of digital literacy and highlights its importance for students in today’s digital world. It includes key topics such as useful digital tools like Google Docs and GitHub, safe internet practices such as using strong passwords and avoiding suspicious links, and email etiquette for professional communication.

The infographic is designed to be simple, visually appealing, and easy to understand for my classmates. I used icons, headings, and short points to make the information clear and engaging. One interesting aspect I found was how effectively visual elements can simplify complex ideas. However, one difficulty I faced was selecting the right amount of content to fit into a single page without making it look overcrowded. Overall, this activity helped me understand the importance of digital literacy and improved my design skills.
